Equity

If you build a Equity, Lending or Reward CrowdBoard you receive the funds once 100% of what you are asking for has been raised, there are no time limits whatsoever. When CrowdBoarders invest money their cards are not charged until the CrowdBoard is funded.

Donation

If you build Gift, Donation or Junior CrowdBoard you receive money in real time until your CrowdBoard reaches 100%. CrowdBoarders cards are charged straight away and money is deposited into the Balanced account you set up when you build your CrowdBoard, this is linked directly to your Bank Account.
